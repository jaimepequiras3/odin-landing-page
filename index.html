Philippine Eagle conservation website
The Philippine Eagle is a critically endangered species of eagle of the family Accipitridae which is endemic to forests in the Philippines.

Philippine Eagle Compilation

It has been declared the national bird of the Philippines.
Has brown and white-colored plumage, a shaggy crest
The largest of the extant eagles in the world in terms of length and wing surface area.
Measures 86 to 102 cm in length and weighs 4.04 to 8 kg.
Also known as the monkey-eating eagle or great Philippine eagle
